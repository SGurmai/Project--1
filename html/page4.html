15th Century

The 15th century witnessed further advancements in armor technology and usage, shaped by the evolving nature of warfare and developments in metallurgy. This period saw refinements in existing armor types as well as the introduction of innovative defensive gear to meet the challenges of combat. Plate armor continued to dominate the battlefield, with improvements in design and construction enhancing both protection and mobility. Additionally, the widespread adoption of firearms necessitated ongoing modifications to armor to withstand ballistic threats. Padded garments such as gambesons remained integral to a warrior's defensive ensemble, providing crucial protection against blunt force trauma. Helmet design also saw continued innovation, with various styles catering to different combat scenarios and preferences.

Helmets

In the 15th century, helmets remained essential pieces of protective equipment for warriors across diverse regions and cultures. Here are some notable helmet types that were prevalent during this period:

Bascinet:

The bascinet continued to be a popular choice for head protection in the 15th century. Featuring a close-fitting skullcap with an attached visor or aventail, bascinets offered improved visibility and ventilation compared to earlier helmet designs.

These examples showcase the diversity of helmet designs during the 15th century, reflecting the ongoing evolution of defensive technology and the varied needs of warriors.

Chest armor

Throughout the 15th century, chest armor remained crucial for protecting the torso and vital organs during combat. Here are some prominent chest armor types from this period:

Plate Cuirass:

The plate cuirass continued to be a staple of 15th-century armor, offering superior protection with articulated metal plates covering the chest and abdomen. This armor provided defense against both slashing and piercing attacks while allowing for freedom of movement.

These examples represent the diverse range of chest armor utilized during the 15th century, showcasing the adaptability and evolution of defensive technologies in response to the demands of warfare.
